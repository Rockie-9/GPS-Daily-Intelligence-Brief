2025 年 11 月，台積電前資深副總羅唯仁被控退休前帶走 2 奈米、A16、A14 等關鍵製程資料投靠英特爾。台積電 11/25 提告，英特爾 11/26 否認。2026 年 4 月，英特爾 18A 良率突飛猛進、Q1 財報盤後大漲 20%。從 X 平台到華爾街都在問同一個問題：技術躍進與羅唯仁加入的時間軸高度重合，這只是巧合嗎？
In Nov 2025, TSMC former SVP Luo Wei-Ren was accused of copying 2nm/A16/A14 process documents before retirement and joining Intel. TSMC filed suit on 11/25; Intel denied on 11/26. By April 2026, Intel's 18A yield jumped unexpectedly; Q1 earnings sent shares up 20%+ after-hours. From X to Wall Street, the same question keeps resurfacing — is the timing coincidence?
為什麼這是今日主信號：這不是新事件，是 6 個月發酵後的關鍵節點。Musk TeraFab 已確認採用英特爾 14A，技術競爭格局正在重組。GPS 不在司法層、不在技術層，但 GPS 是「離職交接」這道流程的設計者——簡報權限、文件帶離權限、競業條款的執行——這些都是 GPS 與 HR/法務交集的場域。
Why this is today's lead: Not new news — it's a 6-month-old story reaching its pivotal moment. Musk's TeraFab has confirmed Intel 14A adoption. Competitive landscape is reshaping. GPS sits not in the courtroom, not in the tech layer — GPS owns offboarding ritual design: briefing access rights, document carry-out rights, non-compete enforcement. This is where GPS intersects with HR and Legal.
GPS 透鏡
GPS LENS
技術機密的載體有兩個——數位文件、活的人。我們花 90% 預算守數位（DLP、加密、權限分級），10% 守人（背景審核、競業條款）。但機密外流的真實案件，過去十年從梁孟松到羅唯仁，都是人帶走的，不是駭客拿走的。GPS 與 HR / 法務應該共同設計：高階主管退休前 60 天的「敏感簡報凍結期」、最後 30 天的「文件影印與帶離稽核」、退休後 24 個月的「外部接觸通報」。
Trade secrets travel in two forms — digital documents and living people. We spend 90% of budget guarding the digital (DLP, encryption, tiered access) and 10% guarding the human side (background checks, non-compete). Yet the actual leak cases of the past decade — from Liang Mong-Song to Luo Wei-Ren — were carried out by people, not stolen by hackers. GPS, HR, and Legal should co-design: 60-day "sensitive briefing freeze" before senior executive retirement; 30-day "document copy and carry-out audit"; 24-month "external contact reporting" post-retirement.
可轉述句
「我們守護機密，不是因為機密重要——是因為信任重要。當高階主管走出大門，他帶走的不只是文件，是同事對體系的信心。」
Cascade sentence
"We guard secrets not because secrets matter — but because trust matters. When a senior executive walks out the door, what they take isn't just documents; it's colleagues' confidence in the system."

羅唯仁案：6 個月後，技術競爭格局正在重組
Luo Wei-Ren case: 6 months on, the competitive landscape is reshaping

事實：台積電前研發副總羅唯仁 2025/11 退休後加入英特爾，台積電 11/25 提告指控他帶走 2 奈米、A16、A14 製程資料，英特爾 11/26 否認。2026/04 英特爾 18A 製程良率在 6 個月內躍升至超預期水準，Q1 財報盤後股價漲逾 20%。Musk 的 TeraFab 已確認採用英特爾 14A。台灣經濟部表示關切國家核心技術管制，配合高檢署釐清是否涉及國安法。

為什麼重要：這不是孤立事件，是台灣半導體人才流動的結構性議題。從梁孟松到羅唯仁到李文如，高階技術主管的流動一再證明：機密外流的主要途徑是人，不是駭客。對 GPS 而言，這重新定義了「資產保護」的範疇——最有價值的資產會自己走出大門，而現行離職流程多半只處理識別證繳回與門禁取消，沒有處理「知識與信任」這個更難的層面。

What happened: TSMC former R&D SVP Luo Wei-Ren joined Intel after retiring in Nov 2025. TSMC filed suit on 11/25 alleging he took 2nm/A16/A14 process data; Intel denied on 11/26. By April 2026, Intel 18A yield jumped to above-target levels within 6 months; Q1 earnings sent shares up 20%+ after-hours. Musk's TeraFab confirmed Intel 14A adoption. Taiwan's MOEA expressed concern over core technology controls, coordinating with prosecutors on potential national security law implications.

Why it matters: This is not isolated — it's a structural issue in Taiwan's semiconductor talent mobility. From Liang Mong-Song to Luo Wei-Ren to Li Wen-Ju, senior technical moves repeatedly prove: the primary path for secret leakage is people, not hackers. For GPS, this redefines "asset protection" — the most valuable assets walk out on their own, while current offboarding mostly handles badge return and access revocation, not the harder layer of "knowledge and trust."

GPS 解讀：GPS 不在司法層，但在「流程設計層」。建議與 HR、法務共同檢視三個現行流程缺口：(1) 高階主管退休前的敏感簡報權限是否有「凍結期」？(2) 最後在職期間的文件下載、列印、外接儲存是否有加強稽核？(3) 退休後的競業條款執行與外部接觸通報機制是否落實？這三項是 GPS 可主動發起、與其他部門協作的具體價值點。
GPS Lens: GPS isn't in the judicial layer, but it is in the "process design layer." Recommend reviewing three gaps with HR and Legal: (1) Is there a "freeze period" on sensitive briefing access before senior executive retirement? (2) Is there enhanced audit of document downloads, printing, external storage during the final employment period? (3) Are post-retirement non-compete enforcement and external contact reporting actually implemented? These are concrete value points GPS can proactively initiate.
建議行動：與 HR、法務發起一次「高階主管離職流程」檢視會議，把上述三個缺口列為議程。GPS 帶一頁 v0.1 草稿入會——不是要主導，是要展現 GPS 從「事件處理」轉向「流程設計者」的價值。
Recommended action: Initiate a "senior executive offboarding process" review with HR and Legal, putting the three gaps on the agenda. GPS brings a one-page v0.1 draft — not to dominate, but to demonstrate GPS shifting from "incident handler" to "process designer."
可轉述句：「我們守護機密，不是因為機密重要——是因為信任重要。當高階主管走出大門，他帶走的不只是文件，是同事對體系的信心。」
Cascade sentence: "We guard secrets not because secrets matter — but because trust matters. When a senior executive walks out the door, what they take isn't just documents; it's colleagues' confidence in the system."

AI Governance Watch · Story 023 SOURCES · OFFICIAL
五眼聯盟發布 AI 代理人指引——要求防範權限擴張與自主行動風險
Five Eyes releases AI agent guidance — mandating defenses against privilege escalation

事實：五眼聯盟（美英加澳紐）於 2026 年 5 月發布 AI 代理人安全指引，核心要求企業防範代理的「權限擴張」與「未授權自主行動」風險。指引呼應 OpenAI、Anthropic、Google DeepMind 三方研究共識的負責任代理設計原則，最核心兩條為「最小權限原則」（代理只擁有當前任務所需最小權限、完成後自動撤銷）與「透明性原則」（決策過程與行動日誌對授權人員完全可見）。此指引與 5/20 NSA 的 MCP 安全指引形成連續的政府級訊號。

為什麼重要：兩個政府級訊號在一個月內連續發布（NSA MCP + 五眼代理指引），意味「AI 代理治理」已從產業自律進入國家安全議程。對 GPS 最直接的意義：當 GPS 內部開始使用 AI 代理自動化（如本簡報自動生成、巡邏排程、事件分流），這些代理本身就是需要被治理的對象。最小權限與行動日誌不再是「IT 的事」，是 GPS 作為流程設計者必須內建的控制。

What happened: The Five Eyes alliance released AI agent security guidance in May 2026, requiring enterprises to defend against agent "privilege escalation" and "unauthorized autonomous action." It echoes the responsible-agent design principles from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind research, with the two most central being "least privilege" (agents hold only minimum privileges, auto-revoked on completion) and "transparency" (decision processes and action logs fully visible to authorized personnel). This forms a continuous government signal alongside NSA's 5/20 MCP guidance.

Why it matters: Two government-level signals within a month mean "AI agent governance" has moved from industry self-regulation onto the national security agenda. The most direct meaning for GPS: when GPS uses AI agents internally (this brief's auto-generation, patrol scheduling, incident triage), these agents become objects requiring governance. Least privilege and action logging are no longer "IT's business" — they are controls GPS must build in as a process designer.

GPS 解讀：GPS 若已使用或計畫使用任何 AI 代理工具，建議建立一份「代理權限清冊」：每個代理能存取什麼資料、能執行什麼動作、誰可查看其行動日誌、任務完成後權限如何撤銷。這份清冊不需技術背景就能起草——它本質是「治理問題」而非「技術問題」，正好是 GPS 的強項。
GPS Lens: If GPS uses or plans to use any AI agent tools, recommend establishing an "agent privilege inventory": what data each agent accesses, what actions it performs, who views its logs, how privileges are revoked on completion. This requires no technical background to draft — it is fundamentally a "governance issue" not a "technical issue," exactly GPS's strength.

Why it matters: This is the scenario closest to employees' daily lives under GPS Pillar 06 Personal Safety. GPS Lens: Recommend a minimal "Stop-Look-Listen" card: on receiving abnormal executive instructions (transfers, cards, confidential transactions), (1) Stop — normal business won't urgently demand money via personal LINE; (2) Look — does it use pressure scripts; (3) Listen — verify with the executive via another known channel. Key message: "Verifying won't offend the executive — being scammed will."
建議行動：製作「停看聽」一頁卡片，1 週內透過各區 Section Heads 推送給高風險族群（財務、行政、採購）。卡片不需要長，核心是給員工一個「不會被究責的查證理由」。
