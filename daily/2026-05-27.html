When secrets walk out as people, GPS guards the offboarding ritual, not the database

2025 年 11 月，台積電前資深副總羅唯仁被控退休前帶走 2 奈米、A16、A14 等關鍵製程資料投靠英特爾。台積電 11/25 提告，英特爾 11/26 否認。2026 年 4 月，英特爾 18A 良率突飛猛進、Q1 財報盤後大漲 20%。從 X 平台到華爾街都在問同一個問題：技術躍進與羅唯仁加入的時間軸高度重合，這只是巧合嗎？
In Nov 2025, TSMC former SVP Luo Wei-Ren was accused of copying 2nm/A16/A14 process documents before retirement and joining Intel. TSMC filed suit on 11/25; Intel denied on 11/26. By April 2026, Intel's 18A yield jumped unexpectedly; Q1 earnings sent shares up 20%+ after-hours. From X to Wall Street, the same question keeps resurfacing — is the timing coincidence?
為什麼這是今日主信號：這不是新事件，是 6 個月發酵後的關鍵節點。Musk TeraFab 已確認採用英特爾 14A，技術競爭格局正在重組。GPS 不在司法層、不在技術層，但 GPS 是「離職交接」這道流程的設計者——簡報權限、文件帶離權限、競業條款的執行——這些都是 GPS 與 HR/法務交集的場域。
Why this is today's lead: Not new news — it's a 6-month-old story reaching its pivotal moment. Musk's TeraFab has confirmed Intel 14A adoption. Competitive landscape is reshaping. GPS sits not in the courtroom, not in the tech layer — GPS owns offboarding ritual design: briefing access rights, document carry-out rights, non-compete enforcement. This is where GPS intersects with HR and Legal.
GPS 透鏡
GPS LENS
技術機密的載體有兩個——數位文件、活的人。我們花 90% 預算守數位（DLP、加密、權限分級），10% 守人（背景審核、競業條款）。但機密外流的真實案件，過去十年從梁孟松到羅唯仁，都是人帶走的，不是駭客拿走的。GPS 與 HR / 法務應該共同設計：高階主管退休前 60 天的「敏感簡報凍結期」、最後 30 天的「文件影印與帶離稽核」、退休後 24 個月的「外部接觸通報」。
Trade secrets travel in two forms — digital documents and living people. We spend 90% of budget guarding the digital (DLP, encryption, tiered access) and 10% guarding the human side (background checks, non-compete). Yet the actual leak cases of the past decade — from Liang Mong-Song to Luo Wei-Ren — were carried out by people, not stolen by hackers. GPS, HR, and Legal should co-design: 60-day "sensitive briefing freeze" before senior executive retirement; 30-day "document copy and carry-out audit"; 24-month "external contact reporting" post-retirement.
可轉述句
「我們守護機密，不是因為機密重要——是因為信任重要。當高階主管走出大門，他帶走的不只是文件，是同事對體系的信心。」
Cascade sentence
"We guard secrets not because secrets matter — but because trust matters. When a senior executive walks out the door, what they take isn't just documents; it's colleagues' confidence in the system."

C.C. Wei 召開全體溝通會議——員工分紅爭議成為品牌信任的關鍵時刻
C.C. Wei convenes all-staff townhall — employee bonus controversy becomes a brand-trust pivot point

事實：董事長 C.C. Wei 於今日（5/27）上午召開全體溝通會議，親自回應自 5/23 起在 Dcard 與 Facebook 員工社群中發酵的「分紅縮減」爭議（Digitimes 5/27）。背景：Q1 2026 淨利 NT$5,725 億創歷史新高、年增 58%，但傳出 2025 績效獎金將縮減 15%，部分報導指 Wei 曾表示「現行分紅水準太高、引發社會負面觀感」、提議降 20-30%（Wccftech、SemiVision 5/24 推文）。員工於 5/24 起在 Liberty Times、TVBS、UPI 等媒體上集體表達不滿，並援引三星電子半導體部門近期工會協議——以營業利益 10.5% 股票 + 1.5% 現金、10 年期、平均每人約 USD 340K 為比較基準。TSMC 自 1987 建廠以來無工會（Tom's Hardware、Seoul Economic Daily 5/24-25）。

為什麼重要：此事件超越分紅金額本身，是「組織信任度」的指標時刻。三層意涵：(1) 員工自發以社群媒體串連、援引競爭對手的勞動模型——意味員工已將自己納入「全球半導體勞動市場」的比較框架，不再僅是 TSMC 內部敘事；(2) 1987 年建廠的「無工會傳統」面臨 39 年來最強挑戰，無論本次是否真組工會，已永久改變勞資對話的基線；(3) 公司於 5/25 發出兩點聲明承諾分紅成長將超過 2025（Digitimes、Tom's Hardware），今早溝通會的執行細節決定信任修復的速度。對 GPS 而言，這是 Pillar 04 Living Culture「安全日常」與 Pillar 03 Family Extension「守護家人」的真實壓力測試——員工對組織的信任，直接影響他們對安全提醒、訓練、合規行為的接受度。

What happened: Chairman C.C. Wei convened an all-staff communication meeting this morning (May 27) to personally address the bonus-cut controversy that has been brewing on Dcard and Facebook employee forums since May 23 (Digitimes May 27). Context: Q1 2026 net profit of NT$572.5B set a new record at +58% YoY, but rumors emerged that 2025 performance bonuses would be cut 15%, with some reports suggesting Wei had earlier stated bonus levels were "too high and create negative perception in society," proposing a 20-30% reduction (Wccftech, SemiVision May 24 tweet). Employees mobilized on May 24 across Liberty Times, TVBS, UPI, citing Samsung's semiconductor union deal as benchmark — 10.5% operating profit in stock + 1.5% cash over 10 years, averaging ~USD 340K per employee. TSMC has operated without a union since 1987 (Tom's Hardware, Seoul Economic Daily May 24-25).

Why it matters: This event transcends the bonus amount itself — it's a moment of "organizational trust" measurement. Three layers: (1) employees mobilized via social media and cited competitors' labor models, meaning they have positioned themselves within the "global semiconductor labor market" frame, no longer confined to TSMC's internal narrative; (2) the union-free tradition since 1987 faces its strongest challenge in 39 years — regardless of whether a union actually forms this time, the baseline for labor-management dialogue has permanently shifted; (3) the company issued a two-point statement on May 25 committing 2026 bonus growth to exceed 2025 (Digitimes, Tom's Hardware), making this morning's townhall execution details decisive for trust-repair speed. For GPS, this is a real stress test of Pillar 04 Living Culture and Pillar 03 Family Extension — employee trust directly affects their receptiveness to safety reminders, training, and compliance behavior.

GPS 解讀：此議題不在 GPS 直接職責範圍，但「員工士氣」是接下來 6 週所有 GPS 溝通的隱性背景。三件事可立即準備：(1) 今早溝通會結果出爐後，即時調整本週 Family Extension 推送的「同理基調」——避免在員工情緒未平時推送 BCP 演練要求；(2) 對 9 區 Section Heads 統一準備一份「對話備忘」——當員工問起分紅議題時，明確 GPS 角色邊界（我們不討論薪酬，但關心您）；(3) 與 HR 確認，是否有 GPS 可協作的「員工關懷」溝通管道——本事件後續可能升溫，需做好預備。
GPS Lens: This issue is not in GPS's direct remit, but "employee morale" is the implicit backdrop for all GPS communications over the next 6 weeks. Three immediate preparations: (1) once the townhall outcome lands, adjust the empathetic tone of this week's Family Extension push — avoid pushing BCP drill requirements while emotions remain raw; (2) prepare a unified "talking points memo" for Section Heads across 9 regions — when employees raise the bonus issue, clearly define GPS's role boundary (we don't discuss compensation, but we care about you); (3) coordinate with HR on whether there's a GPS-collaboration channel for employee care — escalation is possible, prepare in advance.
建議行動：下午之前完成兩件事：(1) 與直屬 GPS Director 確認，本週 GPS 對全員的溝通是否需要調整語氣或時程；(2) 為 9 區 Section Heads 撰寫一份 100 字內的「員工對話備忘」，明確 GPS 的角色邊界與同理態度。
Recommended action: Two things before this afternoon: (1) confirm with the GPS Director whether the week's all-staff GPS communications need tone or timing adjustments; (2) draft a <100-word "employee conversation memo" for the 9-region Section Heads, clearly defining GPS's role boundary and empathetic stance.
可轉述句：「當員工開始用三星比較我們，意味著他們已經把自己放進全球市場，不再只是台積內部的員工。GPS 守護的，是他們對組織的信任。」
Cascade sentence: "When employees start benchmarking us against Samsung, they've already positioned themselves in the global market — not just TSMC's internal workforce. What GPS guards is their trust in the organization."
